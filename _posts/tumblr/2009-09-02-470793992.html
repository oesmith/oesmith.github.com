I’ve been fiddling with OpenCV on-and-off in my spare time for a few weeks now, and I’m starting to get a feel for the challenges involve in number plate recognition. There’s plenty of papers and background material out there to keep me reading for months clicky.

The simple approach as used by a number of researchers is to concentrate on thresholding the image (sometimes many levels of thresholding), and then doing a bit of connected component labelling to find candidate regions of the image to extract and pass to a second-stage character recognition algorithm.
